Push Pull Legs (PPL) Weekly Plan

This PPL split hits every muscle twice a week with a rest day for recovery. Push, pull, and legs are cycled for maximum frequency. Train every set to failure with strict form.

Day 1 – Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Incline Chest Press (Dumbbell, Barbell, or Smith) – 2 sets to failure
  • Chest Fly (Cable or Machine) – 2 sets to failure
  • Tricep Overhead Extension – 2 sets to failure
  • Tricep Pushdown – 2 sets to failure
  • Cable Lateral Raises – 2 sets to failure
  • Shoulder Press – 2 sets to failure

Day 2 – Pull (Back, Biceps, Rear Delts)

  • Lat Pulldowns – 2 sets to failure
  • Wide Grip Rows (Smith, Cable, or Barbell) – 2 sets to failure
  • Close Grip Rows (Dumbbell or Cable) – 2 sets to failure
  • Rear Delt Fly or Face Pulls – 2 sets to failure
  • Cable Bicep Curls – 2 sets to failure
  • Preacher Curls – 2 sets to failure
  • Shrugs – 1 set to failure

Day 3 – Legs

  • Leg Press – 2 sets to failure
  • Leg Extensions – 2 sets to failure
  • Hamstring Curls – 2 sets to failure
  • Calf Raises – 2 sets to failure
  • Hip Thrusts – 2 sets to failure
  • Cable Crunches – 2 sets to failure

Day 4 – Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Chest Press (Machine or Flat Bench) – 2 sets to failure
  • Chest Fly (Cable or Machine) – 2 sets to failure
  • Shoulder Press (Dumbbell, Machine, Smith, or Barbell) – 2 sets to failure
  • Lateral Raises – 2 sets to failure
  • Dipping Movement – 2 sets to failure
  • Unilateral Tricep Pushdowns – 2 sets to failure

Day 5 – Pull (Back, Biceps, Rear Delts)

  • Wide Grip Rows (Smith, Barbell, or Cable) – 2 sets to failure
  • Vertical Lat Pulldown or Pull-Ups – 2 sets to failure
  • Bicep Curls (Cable, Barbell, or Dumbbell) – 2 sets to failure
  • Preacher Curls – 2 sets to failure
  • Hammer Curls (Cable or Dumbbell) – 2 sets to failure
  • Face Pulls or Rear Delt Flies – 2 sets to failure

Day 6 – Legs

  • Barbell or Smith Machine Squats – 2 sets to failure
  • Romanian Deadlifts (RDLs) – 2 sets to failure
  • Hamstring Curls – 2 sets to failure
  • Leg Extensions – 2 sets to failure
  • Calf Raises – 2 sets to failure
  • Cable Crunches – 2 sets to failure

Day 7 – Rest & Recovery

Full rest day. Focus on sleep, eating well, and letting your muscles recover before restarting the cycle.
